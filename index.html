Happy 7th month of landian mahal

(P.S. ikaw yung bigaon talaga)

It's been a month since you came into my life,
A gentle spark that set my world alight.
In every moment, there's a trace of you,
A whisper of a dream that's coming true.

Your laughter, like a melody, so sweet,
Echoes in my heart, a joyful beat.
Your smile, a sunrise in the morning light,
Guides me through the day, so warm and bright.

In every glance, I see a future there,
A place where we can shed our every care.
Your presence, like a gentle, calming breeze,
Brings comfort, puts my restless soul at ease.

A month may seem so brief, so short a time,
Yet in your gaze, I've found a perfect rhyme.
With every day, my feelings grow and bloom,
A garden of affection, full and true.

In shadows deep, where light had scarce a trace,
You found me there, in a dark, silent place.
With gentle words, you pulled me from despair,
And showed me love, a reason to repair.

The road ahead, a path yet to explore,
With hints of roses knocking at your door.
A journey lies, with secret steps to take,
Unspoken words, decisions yet to make.

So here's to you, the one who's captured me,
In just a month, you've made my heart believe.
In love's sweet song, we are the melody,
A tune of joy, of hope, of destiny.

As we journey on, one month and more to come,
Know that with you, my heart is truly home.
For in this month, you've been my saving grace,
A love that fills my life with light and grace.
